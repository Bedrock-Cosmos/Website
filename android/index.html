Bedrock Cosmos Android

We're hoping to release official Android support soon, but we don't have a firm timeline to share yet. Any announcements will be made first on our Discord server and here on this page.

Current Status

Completed
Windows version stable

The Windows launcher is live, open-source, and actively maintained with regular feature updates.

Completed
Android feasibility research

We've confirmed Android support is achievable. A community contributor (Wadudi) has already built an unofficial proof-of-concept.

In Progress
Official Android build

Active development on an official, supported Android release. No ETA — we'll announce when it's ready.

Upcoming
Public release

Once stable and tested, we'll release the official Android version and list it here on this page.

⚠ Warning Please be aware of other people and/or services offering mobile support for Bedrock Cosmos currently! We cannot confirm the safety of other third-party forks or software. The only Android build that is confirmed safe is by Wadudi, who is listed in our Team page as a Contributor. However, this is still unofficial and not supported by the Bedrock Cosmos team. Any future updates about official Android support will be released on our website.
