Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bedrock Cosmos? +
Bedrock Cosmos is a custom proxy for Minecraft Bedrock Edition that adds custom capes, reveals hidden marketplace packs, restores missing content (capes, animations, and skins) within official packs, and adds a variety of other custom skin packs.
Is Bedrock Cosmos safe to use? +
Yes! Bedrock Cosmos is completely open source and available on GitHub. You can review the code and build it yourself to see exactly how our launcher and proxy service function.
Which platforms does Bedrock Cosmos support? +
Currently, Bedrock Cosmos is only available for Windows. It works with any Minecraft Bedrock Edition client (versions 1.21.120 and above) installed on Windows systems, not including Preview or Education Editions.
Will Bedrock Cosmos get me banned? +
No. Bedrock Cosmos operates as a client-side proxy for cosmetic features only and does not provide any unfair advantages.
Can I upload my own custom capes? +
At this time, only the capes built into the Bedrock Cosmos launcher are available for use. We are working to create an update in the future that will allow you to use your own custom capes. In the meantime, you can apply to become a creator in our Discord server to have your capes featured in the app!
Does Bedrock Cosmos affect game performance? +
No! Bedrock Cosmos is designed to be lightweight and runs in the background using minimal system resources. You won't notice any performance impact while playing.
How do I get support? +
Join our Discord server where our community and support team are ready to help! You can ask questions, report issues, and connect with other Bedrock Cosmos users.
