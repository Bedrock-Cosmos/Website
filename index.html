Frequently Asked Questions
What is Bedrock Cosmos?
Bedrock Cosmos is a free, open-source custom proxy for Minecraft Bedrock Edition on Windows. It adds a large library of custom capes and skin packs not available in the base game, reveals hidden or region-locked Marketplace content, and restores missing features — like capes, animations, and skins — that were removed or broken in official pack updates. It runs as a lightweight background process and requires no special technical knowledge to install.
Is Bedrock Cosmos safe to use?
Yes — Bedrock Cosmos is completely open source and publicly available on GitHub. Anyone can read the full source code, inspect every network request it makes, and even build it from scratch themselves. It does not modify game files, inject code into the game process, or transmit any personal data. The proxy only intercepts cosmetic-related traffic to apply capes and skin packs client-side.
Which platforms does Bedrock Cosmos support?
Bedrock Cosmos currently supports Windows 10 and Windows 11 with Minecraft Bedrock Edition version 26.3 or higher. Preview Edition and Education Edition are not supported. Unofficial community builds for Android exist, but official mobile support is not yet available. We are actively working on expanding platform support in future releases.
Will Bedrock Cosmos get me banned?
No. Bedrock Cosmos operates entirely as a client-side cosmetic proxy and does not provide any gameplay advantages, aimbots, speed hacks, or anything that would violate Minecraft's terms of service. It only affects how capes and skins are displayed on your client. It does not interact with anti-cheat systems and has no history of causing bans among its user base.
Can I upload my own custom capes?
At this time, only the capes built into the Bedrock Cosmos launcher are available for use. We are working on a future update that will let you upload and use your own custom cape designs. In the meantime, if you're a creator interested in having your capes featured in the app for all users to enjoy, you can apply through our Discord server.
Does Bedrock Cosmos affect game performance?
No — Bedrock Cosmos is designed from the ground up to be lightweight. It runs as a small background proxy process and uses negligible CPU and memory. It does not inject into the game process or modify any game files, so your FPS, loading times, and overall gameplay experience remain completely unaffected.
How do I get support?
The best place to get support is our Discord server, where both the development team and a helpful community of users are active. You can report bugs, ask setup questions, request features, and get troubleshooting help. For known issues and release notes, check the Changelogs page. You can also browse our GitHub repository to open issues directly.
When will Bedrock Cosmos be coming to Android?
Official Android support is planned for a future release, though we don't have a firm timeline yet. Our current focus is on improving stability and adding new features to the Windows version first. Unofficial community builds for Android do exist, but these are not supported by the Bedrock Cosmos team. Follow our Discord or GitHub for announcements when Android support becomes available.
