How to Get the Ghast Station Skin Pack in Minecraft Bedrock

A new Skin Pack is coming to Minecraft Bedrock, bundled inside an upcoming LEGO set built around one of the game's most recognizable mobs. This covers everything you need to know about the Ghast Station Skin Pack, including how to try all 4 skins early for free through Bedrock Cosmos.

What is the Ghast Station Skin Pack?

The Ghast Station Skin Pack is an upcoming Skin Pack for Minecraft Bedrock Edition that comes bundled as a digital bonus inside LEGO Minecraft set #21597, Ghast Station. Unlike Character Creator items, this is a full skin pack. Each of the 4 skins replaces your whole character look rather than being mixed and matched in the dressing room.

Each skin is based on one of the LEGO minifigures in the set, so you get that same blocky minifigure style brought straight into Minecraft Bedrock.

The LEGO Set: #21597 Ghast Station

The Ghast Station Skin Pack comes with a single redeem code printed inside LEGO Minecraft set #21597, Ghast Station. The set is coming soon at around $99.99 USD and packs in 754 pieces centered on one of Minecraft's most iconic mobs, the Ghast. It includes detailed builds and a handful of minifigures to go with it.

How to Redeem the Ghast Station Skin Pack

Once you have the set, redeeming the code takes about two minutes. Follow the steps below and all 4 skins will show up in your skin library straight away.

1
Get the LEGO set: Purchase LEGO Minecraft #21597 Ghast Station (coming soon, ~$99.99 USD). The single-use redeem code is printed inside the box.
2
Go to the redeem page: Visit minecraft.net/en-us/redeem and sign in with the Microsoft account linked to your Minecraft profile.
3
Enter your code: Type in the code exactly as printed and confirm the redemption. The Ghast Station Skin Pack will unlock to your account immediately.
4
Equip a skin: Launch Minecraft Bedrock, open your skin library, and choose any of the 4 Ghast Station skins to equip.
Note: The redeem code is a single-use, one-time code that unlocks the full skin pack on one account only. Terms and conditions apply. Offer not available in China.

Try Them Early with Bedrock Cosmos

If you don't want to wait for the LEGO set to come out, Bedrock Cosmos lets you use the Ghast Station Skin Pack before it's officially available, completely free. Here's how to do it:

1
Open Bedrock Cosmos: If you don't have it yet, download it from bedrock-cosmos.app.
2
Open the Ghast Station Skin Pack page: Go to the Ghast Station Skin Pack page and click View This Item.
3
Open in Minecraft: Clicking View This Item will open the skin pack directly inside Minecraft Bedrock, letting you equip and use any of the 4 skins for free. No LEGO set needed.

The Ghast Station Skin Pack is a solid addition for anyone who loves the mob-inspired side of Minecraft cosmetics. Whether you pick up the LEGO set when it drops or check it out early through Bedrock Cosmos, the 4 skins are worth grabbing.
