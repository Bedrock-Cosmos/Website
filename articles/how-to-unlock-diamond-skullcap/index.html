How to Get the Diamond Skullcap in Minecraft Bedrock

A new uncommon headwear item is coming to the Minecraft Bedrock Character Creator, bundled with an upcoming LEGO set. Here is everything you need to know, plus how to try it on early using Bedrock Cosmos before anyone else.

What is the Diamond Skullcap?

The Diamond Skullcap is an upcoming Character Creator headwear item for Minecraft Bedrock Edition, classified as Uncommon rarity. It is modelled after the iconic diamond helmet texture, one of the most recognisable looks in all of Minecraft, reimagined as a wearable cosmetic for your character's dressing room.

Diamond Skullcap

The LEGO Set: #21594 The Skeleton

The Diamond Skullcap unlocks through a redeem code included inside LEGO Minecraft set #21594, The Skeleton, coming soon at an estimated retail price of $44.99 USD. The set contains 502 pieces and builds a large-scale Skeleton figure complete with a bow and diamond helmet.

How to Redeem the Diamond Skullcap

Once you have the LEGO set in hand, redeeming the code takes less than two minutes. Follow the steps below and the Diamond Skullcap will appear in your Character Creator headwear section right away.

1
Get the LEGO set: Purchase LEGO Minecraft #21594 The Skeleton (coming soon, ~$44.99 USD). The single-use redeem code is printed inside the box.
2
Go to the redeem page: Visit minecraft.net/en-us/redeem and sign in with the Microsoft account linked to your Minecraft profile.
3
Enter your code: Type in the code exactly as printed and confirm the redemption. The Diamond Skullcap will unlock to your account immediately.
4
Equip it: Launch Minecraft Bedrock, open your Character Creator, navigate to Headwear, and select the Diamond Skullcap to equip it.
Note: The redeem code is a single-use, one-time code that grants the Diamond Skullcap to one account only. Terms and conditions apply. Offer not available in China.

Try It On Early with Bedrock Cosmos

Can't wait for the LEGO set to release? Bedrock Cosmos lets you preview the Diamond Skullcap and other upcoming redeemable items before they are officially available, completely free. Here's how:

1
Open Bedrock Cosmos: If you don't have it yet, download it from bedrock-cosmos.app.
2
Go to Edit Character: Open the Edit Character section from the Bedrock Cosmos main menu.
3
Open Redeemable Items: Navigate to the Redeemable Items tab. This section lets you try on the newest upcoming items first, including the Diamond Skullcap, before anyone else can access them.

The Diamond Skullcap is one of the more eye-catching headwear items coming to Minecraft Bedrock's Character Creator. Whether you grab it through the LEGO set at launch or preview it early via Bedrock Cosmos, it's a clean cosmetic worth having in your dressing room.
