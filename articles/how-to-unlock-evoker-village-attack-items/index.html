How to Get the Evoker Hooded Mantle & Vex Plumed Wings in Minecraft Bedrock

Two new Character Creator items are coming to Minecraft Bedrock, bundled together with an upcoming LEGO set. Here is everything you need to know about the Evoker Hooded Mantle and the Vex Plumed Wings, plus how to try them on early using Bedrock Cosmos.

What are the Evoker Hooded Mantle & Vex Plumed Wings?

The Evoker Hooded Mantle and Vex Plumed Wings are two upcoming Character Creator items for Minecraft Bedrock Edition, coming bundled together in a single LEGO set redemption. The Mantle is a Rare Outerwear item styled after the Evoker mob's signature dark robes, golden trim, and blood-red jewel — one of the most detailed outerwear pieces to hit the Character Creator. The Vex Plumed Wings are a Rare Back Item inspired by the ghostly Vex creatures the Evoker summons, bringing their wispy, ethereal energy to your dressing room.

The LEGO Set: #21596 Evoker Village Attack

Both the Evoker Hooded Mantle and the Vex Plumed Wings unlock through a single redeem code included inside LEGO Minecraft set #21596, Evoker Village Attack, coming soon at an estimated retail price of $89.99 USD. The set contains 607 pieces and recreates the chaos of an Evoker's raid on a Minecraft village, featuring multiple minifigures and detailed village structures.

How to Redeem the Evoker Hooded Mantle & Vex Plumed Wings

Once you have the LEGO set in hand, redeeming the code takes less than two minutes. Follow the steps below and both items will appear in your Character Creator right away.

1
Get the LEGO set: Purchase LEGO Minecraft #21596 Evoker Village Attack (coming soon, ~$89.99 USD). The single-use redeem code is printed inside the box.
2
Go to the redeem page: Visit minecraft.net/en-us/redeem and sign in with the Microsoft account linked to your Minecraft profile.
3
Enter your code: Type in the code exactly as printed and confirm the redemption. Both the Evoker Hooded Mantle and Vex Plumed Wings will unlock to your account immediately.
4
Equip them: Launch Minecraft Bedrock, open your Character Creator, find the Evoker Hooded Mantle under Outerwear and the Vex Plumed Wings under Back Items to equip them.
Note: The redeem code is a single-use, one-time code that unlocks both items on one account only. Terms and conditions apply. Offer not available in China.

Try Them On Early with Bedrock Cosmos

Can't wait for the LEGO set to release? Bedrock Cosmos lets you preview the Evoker Hooded Mantle, Vex Plumed Wings, and other upcoming redeemable items before they are officially available, completely free. Here's how:

1
Open Bedrock Cosmos: If you don't have it yet, download it from bedrock-cosmos.app.
2
Go to Edit Character: Open the Edit Character section from the Bedrock Cosmos main menu.
3
Open Redeemable Items: Navigate to the Redeemable Items tab. This section lets you try on the newest upcoming items first, including the Evoker Hooded Mantle and Vex Plumed Wings, before anyone else can access them.

The Evoker Hooded Mantle and Vex Plumed Wings are two of the most thematic Character Creator items coming to Minecraft Bedrock — perfect for anyone who loves the illager aesthetic. Whether you grab them through the LEGO set at launch or preview them early via Bedrock Cosmos, they're a bold pair worth adding to your dressing room.
